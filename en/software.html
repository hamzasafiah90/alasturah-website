API & Webhook Integration

Merging security and other systems with your data network means one connected ecosystem instead of isolated tools. We build integrations on open APIs and webhooks so your systems share data in real time.

  • Centralized Management: One dashboard pulling data from your different systems, instead of switching between separate panels.
  • Real-Time Sync: Webhook events notify your systems the moment something changes, without resource-heavy polling.
  • Frictionless Integration: Connecting your existing systems (accounting, CRM, security) to each other through documented, secure interfaces.
