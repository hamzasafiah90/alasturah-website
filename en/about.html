About Us

A Saudi company bringing telecom engineering and software development together under one roof.

Our Story

Shabakat Alasturah (Shabakat Alasturah Almumayizah Establishment) is a Saudi company founded to deliver reliable connectivity solutions for buildings and facilities, alongside custom software development built for the needs of local businesses. We bring together a telecom engineering team and a software development team, so clients get integrated solutions instead of juggling multiple vendors.

What We Do

We operate across three service lines. Network solutions cover 5G boosters, centralized satellite systems, and enterprise infrastructure including fiber, wireless, and data centers. Physical security covers AI video intelligence and access control systems. Software development covers custom applications and systems built around how a business actually works, rather than off-the-shelf tools that don't fit.

Our Approach to Quality & Compliance

We make sure the connectivity equipment we supply complies with the regulations of the Communications, Space & Technology Commission (CST) in Saudi Arabia, because unlicensed boosters can degrade mobile networks and expose users to legal liability. This is a core part of how we work, not an optional extra.

Where We Operate

We serve individuals, businesses, and property developers across Saudi Arabia.
