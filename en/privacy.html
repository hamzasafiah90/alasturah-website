Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 27, 2025

This text is drafted around the general requirements of Saudi Arabia's Personal Data Protection Law (PDPL), issued under the supervision of the Saudi Data & Artificial Intelligence Authority (SDAIA), and is a starting draft only. Have it reviewed by a licensed legal advisor in Saudi Arabia before final publication.

1. Who We Are

This policy covers the Shabakat Alasturah website (Shabakat Alasturah Almumayizah Establishment). For any privacy-related question, contact us at support@alasturah.com.

2. Data We Collect

  • Data you submit through the contact form: name, email, phone number, and message content.
  • Basic technical data generated automatically when visiting the site (such as browser type), if analytics tools are added later.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To respond to your inquiries and quote requests.
  • To contact you regarding the services or products you requested.
  • To improve our website and services.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Limited data may be shared with technical service providers (such as email or hosting providers) only to the extent necessary to deliver our services.

5. Your Rights Under the PDPL

  • The right to know what data we hold about you.
  • The right to request correction of your data.
  • The right to request deletion of your data, within legal limits.
  • The right to file a complaint with the Saudi Data & Artificial Intelligence Authority (SDAIA).

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@alasturah.com.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as necessary to fulfil the purpose it was collected for, or as required by law.

7. Updates to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any update will be published on this page with a revised date.
